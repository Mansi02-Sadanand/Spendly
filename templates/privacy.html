Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes:

Account Information: Your email address and password when you create an account.

Financial Data: Expense transactions you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

Usage Data: How you interact with our service, such as features you use and pages you visit.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

• Provide, maintain, and improve our expense tracking services

• Process your transactions and send you related information

• Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages

• Respond to your comments and questions

• Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We use encryption to protect data in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party companies to perform services on our behalf, such as hosting and data storage.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

• Access the personal information we hold about you

• Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data

• Request deletion of your personal information

• Export your data in a portable format

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities. Cookies help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our service.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the service or by email. Your continued use of Spendly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to resolving any concerns you may have.
